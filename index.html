Creating websites since 2007

I’ve been creating websites for sole traders, businesses and organisations since 2007. Since I started, I have had a lot of trial and error and the digital world has changed a lot. I have learned how to navigate the online tools available and create processes that work.

I started my career with a degree in graphic design. After I graduated, I realised that to get the high quality that I wanted, I'd need to learn to code. After a slightly bumpy start, I now teach others how to write HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
