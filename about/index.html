I’m Lorinda (Lori for short), a web designer in Leicester.

As well as designing, I code. With this rare combination of skills, I create websites that look good and perform well.

I can help you if your website looks unprofessional or no longer represents you. Or get in touch if you’re not sure why you hardly ever get enquiries from your website. You deserve a website that helps you achieve your goals.

Creating websites since 2007

Since I started, I have had a lot of trial and error and the digital world has changed a lot. I have learned how to navigate the online tools available and create processes that work.

My skills and experience are tailored to help service providers. I was the in-house graphic designer and website designer for a training and recruitment provider for seven years. I then worked at a design agency where I created websites and other marketing materials for B2B agencies, charities, tradespeople, clinics, manufacturers, laboratories, and sole traders.

I have a passion for service design, and I design and build microsites. Some of these sites are used for internal company use, for things like nominating the employee of the month.

Continuing professional development

After graduating with a Graphic Design BA (Hons), I realised that to get the high quality that I wanted, I need to learn to code. After a slightly bumpy start, I now teach others how to code using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I’ve also completed training and workshops in copywriting, accessibility, email marketing, pay-per-click advertising, Google Analytics, social media strategy and SEO.

My values

I may not run marathons for charity, but I try to do the right thing. So I’ll be honest with you and won’t sell you something if I don’t think it’s what you need.
