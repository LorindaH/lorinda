Training to get more from your website

Don’t be left confused and overwhelmed about what to do next.

Get Started

My training sessions are carried out remotely on a one-to-one basis.

All levels are hands-on training which is good for learners who want to have a go themselves. You’ll share your screen with me and I’ll talk you through it.

What do the sessions cover?

I tailor the training to your website, but usually cover how to:

  • login and keep your site secure;
  • add blog posts or case studies;
  • add landing pages;
  • edit text on pages;
  • replace images on pages;
  • and edit the site’s menus and footer.

I also run intermediate level sessions that cover how to:

  • use best practices for website content;
  • add videos; and/or
  • use the Yoast SEO plugin for search engine optimisation.

If you like you can also:

  • ask me to record the session;
  • have other people watch the session (so I can answer their questions);
  • bring a list of website changes for us to do together;
  • edit text on pages; and/or
  • let me know if there’s anything extra you’d like me to cover.

How much does it cost?

I charge £49 per hour, and I recommend that you book a two-hour training session. This rate also covers the preparation time that I spend tailoring the session to your needs and creating the agenda.

What does the learner need to do before the session?

Let me know if there’s anything extra you want me to cover at least 24 hours before the session.

You’ll need to join the session using a laptop or desktop device (not a mobile). Make sure there is a microphone on the device so I can hear you (you’ll be sharing your screen with me, so a camera isn’t essential).

Working with agencies

Although I mainly work directly with website owners, I also freelance for agencies. I worked for a design agency for over six years. I am happy to work on your project through an account manager or work directly with your client.
