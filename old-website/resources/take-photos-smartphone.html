Tips for taking photos on a smartphone

  • Turn off the flash and pick the brightest time of day so you have more natural light.
  • Hold your phone straight and parallel to the floor.
  • Take a mixture of landscape and portrait shots.
  • Use both hands to keep your hands steady and help you take a clear image.
  • Focus on one subject – don’t have too much going on in one shot.
  • Avoid zooming in – try to get closer to your subject instead.
