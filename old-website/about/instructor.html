Pass on the knowledge

I volunteer as an instructor to introduce under-represented people to web development.

I was one of the instructors on the Code First: Girls 2019 course in Leicester. This beginners coding course covered HTML, CSS, Git, and JavaScript. There were a group of 20 learners who were students at the University of Leicester. We had a 100% pass rate.

Code First: Girls (CF:G) provides young women with free coding courses to increase the proportion of women in tech. Why is this important? Well, one reason is that a diversity of thought leads to better problem-solving.

Visit www.codefirstgirls.org.uk to find out more.
